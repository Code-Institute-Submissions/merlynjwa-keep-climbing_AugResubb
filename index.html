Who are we?

Keep Climbing is a climbing centre based in Wokingham, Berkshire.

Climbing equipment available

The centre supplies equipment at a nominal fee for use. We can supply harnesses, ropes, belays, carabinas, chalk and pouches, shoes and weight bags.

These supplies can also be purchased from our store.

You may climb using your own equipment provided that it meets our standards for safety.

Why you should climb

Climbing is a great way to exercise nearly every muscle in the body. Through gradual development and practice, climbing will help to improve your grip and control of your balance.

Climbing also serves as a great source of mental exercise. It requires problem solving on the part of the climber in order to navigate the rock face which they are faced with and to decide which techniques are the most efficient and possible for them to use to get to the top.

As well as your body and mind, climbing develops your social bonds. Whether you are joining us on one of our events or come to the centre with a climbing partner, the continual reliance on each other in order to climb safely and with ease builds trust between you and others.
